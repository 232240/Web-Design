Clash Royale was created by Supercell, a studio in Helsinki, Finland. A small team experimented with mixing card games, tower defense, and real-time battles. Using characters from Clash of Clans, they built a faster, competitive game that still felt familiar to Clash fans. After months of testing, they struck the balance between fun, strategy, and mobile play.

Clash Royale is a real-time strategy card game. Players collect and upgrade cards representing troops, spells, and defenses, then battle in live 1v1 or 2v2 matches. The goal is to destroy enemy towers within 3 minutes. With quick matches, unique units, and regular updates, the game blends strategy and fast reactions, keeping it fresh for millions of players.
